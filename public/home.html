Sourcing Simplified

From its genesis as a building and construction procurement tool, (which the name BoB or Brick on Brick came from) has grown into a full function negotiation suite. Covering the electronic delivery of RFQ/RFI/RFP (or simply “eRFx”), reverse auctions and forward auctions; BoB eProcure has the ability to automate procurement selection and negation practices for any business big or small. To date BoB eProcure has mediated over $2 billion of purchase decisions.
